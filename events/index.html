EMBERS 2023

P.A College of Engineering, Dept of CSE organizes 2 days Embedathon and project exhibition under Embed Club on 28th and 29th of April 2023. Embers is a national level technical fest which aims to solve problems and give a platform to showcase the project. Embers will be conducted at PACE campus.

Day-1 Embedathon:

Embadathon is a unique student event that challenges you to design and build embedded projects

Embadathon offers four major domains for you to choose from:

Agriculture: Create smart solutions for the agricultural industry.

Healthcare: Develop embedded projects that can revolutionize healthcare

Education: Empower the future of education with embedded solutions

Home Automation: Make homes smarter with embedded projects

At Embadathon, you not only get to design and build your embedded project, but you also have the opportunity to showcase it during the project expo for free. Share your innovative solutions with fellow participants and judges and compete for the top spot in the contest! The winners will be selected based on innovation, creativity, and impact of their embedded projects.

Rules:

  • Each team has to select any one topic from the above mentioned.
  • Open for all students.
  • Carry all the components and requirements for the event.
  • All work should be done during the Embedathon.
  • Teams can use an idea they had before the event.
  • Innovative projects are entertained.
  • Teams should stop working once the time is up, however they can do small modification on next day.
  • Breaking of rules will lead to disqualification.

Day-2
Project Exhibition:

Project Expo is a thrilling event that offers students a unique platform to demonstrate their revolutionary software, hardware, or embedded projects.

It's a moment to shine as you present your projects through live demos, interactive displays, and engaging interactions with visitors. Discuss the challenges faced, lessons learned, and the impact your project can make. Elevate your project presentation with compelling PPTs that complement your projects and leave a lasting impression.

Use visually appealing slides, diagrams, and flowcharts to convey the essence of your project. From the introduction to the block diagram, working principle, advantages, disadvantages, and future work, your PPT can be a powerful visual aid that effectively communicates your ideas to the audience.

Rules:

  • Each team can have maximum 4 members.
  • B.E/PolyTechnic/BCA/B.tech students are eligible for attending the event.
  • A team member can only be part of one project.
  • Project must be presented with an effective PPT with the prototype.
  • Participants should exhibit their project in the event of Embers on 29th April 2023.

Note: Winners will be awarded with exciting prizes.
